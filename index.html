A little bit about me

Hi! I'm currently a Computer Science student at the University of Michigan. I actually went into my freshman year expecting to do mechanical engineering, but after I took an intro programming course, I realized I had far greater interest in software development. When I'm not writing code, you can find me exercising or watching The Expanse.

Recent Work

Since May 2020, I've been a part of BUYMYSPOT, an Ann Arbor startup that provides an online marketplace for parking spots. I've been working on the new mobile app, from its UI design and implementation in React Native to how transactions and authentication are handled on the backend. This has been the largest project I've worked on, and has provided me an invaluable opportunity to learn and express my skills.
